## Purpose and Scope
This Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) defines the standard practices, expectations, and governance for delivering software within the organisation. It applies to all internal teams and external vendors involved in the design, development, integration, and support of software solutions.

The SDLC supports multiple delivery approaches, including Waterfall, Agile (e.g., Scrum), and Product Operating Model (POM), while ensuring consistent controls for quality, security, compliance, and operational readiness.

This SDLC does not prescribe a single way of working; instead, it defines the minimum standards and controls required to ensure consistent, secure, and high-quality delivery. Teams are trusted to operate within these guardrails, with accountability for achieving the required outcomes.
