# Core Roles and Responsibilities

#### Product Manager
The Product Manager is accountable for maximising business value and ensuring alignment between stakeholder needs and delivered outcomes.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Define product vision, objectives, and success metrics
* Own and prioritise the product backlog or requirements
* Engage stakeholders and represent user needs
* Ensure requirements are clear, testable, and aligned to business value
* Accept or reject completed work based on defined criteria
* Drive continuous discovery, including prototyping and user validation
* Balance scope, value, risk, and time-to-market

---

#### Delivery Lead / Dev Lead / Tech Lead / Project Manager / Scrum Master
This role is accountable for facilitating delivery and ensuring the team operates effectively.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Plan and coordinate delivery activities
* Facilitate ceremonies, milestones, or stage gates as appropriate
* Manage timelines, dependencies, and risks
* Remove impediments and support team productivity
* Ensure alignment with SDLC processes and governance requirements
* Provide transparent reporting on progress and issues

---

#### Solution Architect
The Solution Architect is accountable for the overall solution design and alignment with enterprise architecture.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Define and maintain solution architecture
* Ensure alignment with enterprise standards and strategies
* Evaluate technology choices and integration approaches
* Identify and manage technical risks and trade-offs
* Support design governance and architecture reviews
* Provide guidance to development teams

---

#### Developers / Engineers
Developers are responsible for building, integrating, and maintaining the solution.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Develop code in accordance with standards and best practices
* Participate in design and technical decision-making
* Implement automated tests and integration processes
* Conduct peer reviews and ensure code quality
* Maintain version control and documentation
* Address defects and technical debt

---

#### Testers / Quality Assurance (QA)
QA roles ensure that the solution meets defined quality standards.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Define test strategies and plans
* Develop and execute test cases (manual and automated)
* Validate functional and non-functional requirements
* Identify, log, and track defects
* Ensure traceability between requirements and testing
* Support user acceptance testing

---

#### Security, Privacy & Risk
This function ensures that solutions meet organisational security and compliance requirements.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Define security and compliance requirements
* Conduct risk assessments and threat modelling
* Review designs and implementations for security risks
* Support vulnerability management and remediation
* Provide guidance on secure practices

---

#### Operations / Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)
Operations ensures that solutions are deployable, supportable, and reliable in production.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Define operational requirements (monitoring, alerting, scaling)
* Support environment provisioning and management
* Ensure deployment readiness and production support models
* Monitor system performance and availability
* Respond to incidents and support continuous improvement

---

#### Change Manager
The Change Manager governs the introduction of changes into production environments.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Ensure all changes are assessed, authorised, and recorded
* Facilitate change approval processes in line with organisational policy
* Support risk-based change classification (standard, normal, emergency)
* Ensure appropriate stakeholder communication
* Maintain auditability of changes
